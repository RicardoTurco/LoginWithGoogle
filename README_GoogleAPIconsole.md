# Google API Console  

Here you will find the necessary steps to create PROJECT, CUSTOMER ID and SECRET KEY  
in the "Google API Console".     
```(the images used are in Portuguese language)```

```1``` -  Access the URL ```https://console.developers.google.com``` in you browser, and define your EMAIL:  
(if your first access, acept the TERMS. After that, click on "Concordar e Continuar" option.)  

![Alt text](imgs/ConsoleGoogle_001.png?raw=true)

```2``` -   If your first access, click on "CRIAR PROJETO" option:

![Alt text](imgs/ConsoleGoogle_002a.png?raw=true)

```3``` - If not ... click on the drop-down menu where any project will be displayed:  

![Alt text](imgs/ConsoleGoogle_002b.png?raw=true)

```4``` - Click on "NOVO PROJETO" option and after in "ABRIR" option:

![Alt text](imgs/ConsoleGoogle_002c.png?raw=true)

```5``` - Define one name to project, click on "CRIAR" button:

![Alt text](imgs/ConsoleGoogle_003.png?raw=true)

```6``` - After that, click "Tela de consentimento OAuth" option on the left side;  
click on "Externo" option and click in "CRIAR" button:

![Alt text](imgs/ConsoleGoogle_004.png?raw=true)

```7``` - Describe your app, and define your EMAIL:

![Alt text](imgs/ConsoleGoogle_005a.png?raw=true)

```8``` - Click on the "SALVAR" button:

![Alt text](imgs/ConsoleGoogle_005b.png?raw=true)

```9``` - If everything was done correctly:

![Alt text](imgs/ConsoleGoogle_006.png?raw=true)

```10``` - Click "Credenciais" option on the left side and click "CRIAR CREDENCIAIS" option on the "top" region:

![Alt text](imgs/ConsoleGoogle_007a.png?raw=true)

```11``` - Click "ID do cliente do OAuth" option:

![Alt text](imgs/ConsoleGoogle_007b.png?raw=true)

```12``` - Select an a one option in the drop-down menu:  
("Aplicativo da Web" option)

![Alt text](imgs/ConsoleGoogle_008a.png?raw=true)

```13``` - Define "name" to your app:

![Alt text](imgs/ConsoleGoogle_008b.png?raw=true)

```14``` - Define the "URI" and "Redirect URI":

![Alt text](imgs/ConsoleGoogle_008c.png?raw=true)

```15``` - And finally CUSTOMER ID and SECRET KEY will be displayed:

![Alt text](imgs/ConsoleGoogle_009.png?raw=true)
